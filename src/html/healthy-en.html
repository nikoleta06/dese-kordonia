Wellness & Benefits of Running

Few things give so much for so little. Running isn't just exercise — it's a way to care for your body and mind at the same time. A few kilometres are enough to reduce stress, boost your immune system and make you feel stronger inside and out.

Benefits by Body System

Pick what interests you — running helps almost everywhere.

Cardiovascular

  • Lowers cardiovascular disease risk
  • Improves circulation
  • Lowers blood pressure

Brain

  • Better focus
  • Less anxiety
  • Better mood

Lungs

  • Better endurance
  • Increased oxygenation

Bones & Muscles

  • Stronger bones
  • Greater muscular endurance

Sleep

  • Deeper sleep
  • Easier waking up

Weight

  • More calories burned
  • Better metabolism

What you gain after...

The benefits of running build up gradually. Here's what changes over time.

1 run

  • Better mood
  • Endorphin release

1 month

  • Better fitness level
  • Less fatigue

6 months

  • Better cardiorespiratory endurance
  • Lower resting heart rate

1 year

  • Significant health improvement
  • Greater self-confidence

Small habits, big difference

  • Sleep = training Your body grows stronger while it rests, not while it runs. 7–8 hours of sleep make the difference.
  • Hydrate before you're thirsty Drink water throughout the day, not just around your run.
  • Warm-up & stretching Five minutes before and after reduce injuries and help recovery.
