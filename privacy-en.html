Privacy Policy

A quick, plain-language rundown: what data I keep, why, and how you can see or delete it.

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Data controller

The data controller is the creator of Δέσε Κορδόνια.

What data I collect

If you're just browsing the site, I don't ask you for any personal data. The infrastructure providers I use (GitHub Pages, Render, Supabase) may log technical details such as your IP address, as part of the normal operation/security of their servers — I don't have access to those logs and don't use them to identify you. Your "likes" and "saved" articles are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) — they never reach me tied to you, only an anonymous total like count per article.

If you create an account (sign in with an email "magic link", no password), I keep: your email, optionally your name and a short bio if you fill them in, and whether you want to receive the newsletter.

How I use it & legal basis

  • To sign you in via email with no password, and to remember your profile details — necessary to provide the account you asked for.
  • To send you the newsletter — only with your consent, when you opt in.

I don't sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

How long I keep it

Your account details stay stored for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, they're removed from the database immediately.

Where it's stored

Everything I use is hosted within the European Union:

  • Your account (email, name, bio, newsletter preference): database on Supabase (Frankfurt, Germany).
  • The server (API) that manages it: Render (Frankfurt, Germany).
  • Sign-in & newsletter emails are sent via Resend (Ireland).
  • The anonymous like counter, in the same Supabase project — no personal data.
  • Traffic is measured anonymously via GoatCounter, with no cookies and no personal data.

Cookies

I only use one strictly necessary session cookie while you're signed in, so I can recognize you. It expires after 7 days or when you sign out. No advertising, profiling, or third-party tracking cookies.

Your rights

  • View and edit your details anytime on the "My account" page.
  • Delete your account anytime from the same page — this immediately removes your email, name, bio, and newsletter preference from the database.
  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter via the link in every email.

Contact

For any question about your personal data or this Privacy Policy, you can reach out at hello@desekordonia.com.
