Privacy Policy

A quick, plain-language rundown: what data I keep, why, and how you can see or delete it.

Last updated: July 6, 2026

What data I collect

If you're just browsing the site, I don't collect any personal data. Your "likes" and "saved" articles are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) — they never reach me tied to you, only an anonymous total like count per article.

If you create an account (sign in with an email "magic link", no password), I keep: your email, optionally your name and a short bio if you fill them in, and whether you want to receive the newsletter.

How I use it

  • To sign you in via email, with no password.
  • To remember your profile details next time you sign in.
  • To send you the newsletter, only if you've opted in.

I don't sell, share, or use your data for advertising.

Where it's stored

  • Your account (email, name, bio, newsletter preference): stored on Supabase (Frankfurt) — the server (API) that manages it is hosted on Render.
  • Sign-in & newsletter emails are sent via Resend.
  • The anonymous like counter, in the same Supabase project — no personal data.
  • Traffic is measured anonymously via GoatCounter, with no cookies and no personal data.

Cookies

I only use one strictly necessary session cookie while you're signed in, so I can recognize you. It expires after 7 days or when you sign out. No advertising or tracking cookies.

Your rights

  • View and edit your details anytime on the "My account" page.
  • Delete your account — and all your data — anytime, from the same page.
  • Unsubscribe from the newsletter via the link in every email.

Contact

Questions about your data? Find me on Instagram @nikol.akr.
